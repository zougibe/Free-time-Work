How to Use

1. Configure Scenario

Select an industry, role, objective, and optional obstacle to design your training scenario.

2. Run Simulation

Click "Run Scenario" to see an animated simulation of your training scenario.

3. View Results

The effectiveness score reflects how well the scenario is designed. Higher scores indicate better training potential.

4. Save or Export

Save scenarios to your browser or export them as text files for documentation.

Tips for Better Scenarios

  • Include realistic obstacles to test trainees' problem-solving skills
  • Emergency objectives typically yield higher effectiveness scores
  • Random challenges will occasionally appear to test adaptability
